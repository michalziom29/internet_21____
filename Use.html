Use of the internet:

 

The internet can be used in many ways, since it has evolved so much we use it on a daily basis for communications, education or as a way to relax like online gaming.

Communications:

Since the internet sending and receiving emails has been ease in this day and age. No more waiting a week or more for a reply when you can contact a business or a co-worker and get a reply in minutes.

VoIP (Voice over internet protocol) is a way to call someone using the internet, a user has a unique number that can be used to call them using a computer or a phone, without having to pay for it. An example of this would be Skype or WhatsApp.

Information search:  

Searching information has been made so easy for anyone to use and to find any kind of information they need, from tutorials to books or history information. By now, anyone can practically find out anything they would need to know trough the World Wide Web.

File downloading:

Downloading files is easier than ever with many websites offering free software such as antivirus or an open license software. You no longer need to go to shops to purchase music or movies when you can easily purchase them online, most people will have a decent internet speed which turns out much more convenient for most users.  

File sharing:

Sharing files at work or college is simple with apps such as one drive or office 365. File sharing is critical for most businesses in this age as almost everything is kept digitally. 

Research:

The internet's biggest advantage is the fact that anyone can access any information they need to research with ease using the internet. While some websites offer, information for money with a little more searching you could find that information, you need free and fast. Research is vital in a business or college, or even at home and with internet available, at your fingertips it will be easy to find whatever you need.

Online gaming:

While it is not for everyone, many people enjoy playing multiplayer games, with the help of internet it is possible to play simultaneously with friends or complete strangers.

Further information

The internet has evolved the way we purchase items, how we reasearch information and how we interact with eachother.
